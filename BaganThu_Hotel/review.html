Wonderful location, an ideal base for Bagan

The River View is, as might be expected, on the mighty Irrawaddy River. Complete with its own ruined temple, the hotel is a complex comprising blocks housing around twenty rooms. The restaurant offers decent Myanmar food but its big attraction is that it's outside, overlooking the river and facing west so that every evening is a sunset spectacular. Located in Old Bagan, the hotel is within walking distance of the many temples and pagodas in the old town and ideally situated for road travel - by rented e-bike or taxi - to the wider area. Rooms are comfortable but, in our block, the bathroom was in serious need of modernisation. There is a nice swimming pool - but it woukd be even better if the bar was open
